
# Eternal Limited (Formerly Zomato Limited) – Equity Valuation Project

## Overview

This project presents a comprehensive equity valuation of Eternal Limited (formerly Zomato Limited) through an integrated financial modeling and valuation framework.

The project was developed to analyze the company's historical financial performance, forecast future operating results, estimate intrinsic value through a Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) valuation and assess market expectations through Reverse DCF analysis.

The objective was not only to determine what the business may be worth, but also to understand the key drivers influencing long-term value creation.

---

## Project Components

### Financial Modeling

* Historical Income Statement Analysis (FY23–FY25)
* Historical Balance Sheet Analysis (FY23–FY25)
* Historical Cash Flow Statement Analysis (FY23–FY25)
* Integrated Three-Statement Financial Model
* Driver-Based Forecasting

### Valuation

* Free Cash Flow to Firm (FCFF) Valuation
* Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Analysis
* Sensitivity Analysis
* Scenario Analysis (Bear, Base and Bull Cases)
* Reverse DCF Analysis

### Research Report

* Business Overview
* Investment Thesis
* Forecast Assumptions
* Valuation Summary
* Key Risks
* Conclusion

## Key Takeaway

The analysis suggests that Eternal remains a high-quality consumer technology business with significant long-term growth potential through Blinkit's expanding quick-commerce platform.

However, Reverse DCF analysis indicates that the market currently prices in substantially more optimistic growth and profitability assumptions than those used in the base-case valuation, highlighting the importance of distinguishing between business quality and valuation expectations.
